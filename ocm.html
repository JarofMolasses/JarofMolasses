OCM (Opticlaw Mod)


A variable-diameter endoscope test fixture mount for Intuitive Surgical.
With Capstone team: Phoebe Cheung, Cameron Lee-Ming, and Adam Metwally.

In this project, our team created a specialized test fixture mount which holds endoscopes for testing and validation. I was primarily responsible for the creation of the fundamental geometry and the design of the spring-detented locking mechanism.

The concept behind such a mount was already detailed in a Newport patent (expired), but we reverse engineered the mechanism to generate a customized geometry for the endoscopes.


The intrinsic function of the mount is based on the specific geometry of the bottom arm in the clamp, which contains two numerically defined curves. The curves were generated using a python script. The top and bottom arms are coupled using a gear mechanism, and the combination of the arm geometry and the gear ratio ensures that any circle within a range of diameters will be held at the same center.
